All About Barks

Barks remains to be the largest pet supply retail superstore for dogs in the country. It is a one-stop shop for pet needs and more. It has a fantastic selection of pet products, including food, grooming tools, toys, cages and more. It has a wide assortment of brands, carrying both local and imported pet products. It offers modern, spacious and clean salon facility for pet grooming and trained professional groomers. Barks also sells a different breed of dogs.

Barks also offers quality veterinary services provided no less than by licensed veterinarians certified by the Philippine Veterinary Medical Association. Aside from this, Barks has its own pharmacy for your pets' medical needs. Moreover, dog training classes are also made available to pet guardians who want to improve interaction between them and their pets.
